Categories

Categories in event planning classify events based on their purpose, format, and audience. Understanding these categories simplifies the planning process and ensures the right venue, vendors, and activities are selected. Popular categories include corporate events, social events, fundraising events, cultural events, educational events, sports events, and government/political events.

Learn More
Corporate Events

Meetings, conferences, product launches, trade shows, etc.

Social Events

Weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, baby showers, etc.

Cultural Events

Festivals, concerts, art shows, community events, etc

Sports Events

Tournaments, races, competitions, etc.

Fundraising Events

Charity galas, auctions, benefit concerts, etc.

Educational Events

Workshops, seminars, training sessions, etc.
